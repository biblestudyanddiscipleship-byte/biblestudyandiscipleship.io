lean about us

welcome to our bible study and discipleship class site where you can learn and experience the supernatural power of God's word in your life.

We are dedicated to helping people overcome challenges, including imorality, through discipleship and Bible study. We love to guide believers into deeper faith and freedom in Christ which you can achieve through daily devotion with God.

Our Mission

Our mission is to have the word of God hidden in the hearts of men rich and poor, successful and unsuccessful, old and young and any other who longs to know God more.
